## ✍️ Description & Execution
- The direction of the switch is the same as [Dame](/moves/dame)
### Leader
- 123 - [Enchufla](/moves/enchufla)
- 567 - Walk into the middle, and put hands on shoulders making a circle
- 123 567 - Back step
- 123 - [Opposite Vuelta](/moves/opposite-vuelta) towards the new follower
  - Follower starts the [Dile Que No](/moves/dile-que-no) alone
- 567 - Finish the [Dile Que No](/moves/dile-que-no)
### Follower
- 123 - [Enchufla](/moves/enchufla)
- 567 - Go behind the leader and face the circle
- 123 567 - Back step, raise the same arm as the leg going back
- **Keep Eye Contact with your next leader**
- 123 - Start [Dile Que No](/moves/dile-que-no) alone
- 567 - Finish [Dile Que No](/moves/dile-que-no) with the leader
### Easy Version
Extends the Back step count by 4, leaders return with a [Ronde](/moves/ronde), the [Dile Que No](/moves/dile-que-no) is started together
- 123 - [Enchufla](/moves/enchufla)
- 567 - Walk into the middle, and put hands on shoulders making a circle
- 123 567 123 - Back step
- 567 - [Ronde](/moves/ronde) towards the new follower
- 123 567 - [Dile Que No](/moves/dile-que-no)
