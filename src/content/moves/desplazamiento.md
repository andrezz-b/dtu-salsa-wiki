## ✍️ Description & Execution
- Collection of moves where you walk around the dance floor
- More explanation video: 
  - [🇨🇺 Desplazamientos: adding movement to casino](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-bcB2rr2gac)
### Basic Walk
#### Leader
- Hold the followers right with your left, around the wrist (don't grab with all the fingers)
- 123 567 - Walk forwards
- You may exit this by leading the follower to walk to your side and then doing a [Dile Que No](/moves/dile-que-no)
#### Follower
- Right hand palm down, keep a strong frame and tension of the elbow
  - If the leader pushes pulls in some direction you need to move *as a unit*
- 123 567 - Walk Forwards
- During the walk roll your shoulders on the free arm
### Hook Walk (+ side switch)
- Hands *hooked*, similar to [Prima con Familia](/moves/prima-con-familia) but both leader and follower are in the same direction
- You walk in this position, the follower can switch sides, this is described below
#### Leader
- Follower is on my right, she walks to my left
  - Other direction works the same way
- While walking by turning more strongly to the left you signal the move
  - Loosen the hook so the follower can exit it and freely walk
  - You should give the signal around 4 or 8 so that the follower can have a 3 count to go to the other side
  - With your other hand you can show the way
- While the follower is walking turn in the opposite way of her walk (so that she doesn't need to walk as much)
- Open up the left arm so that she can easily connect
#### Follower
- When given the impulse imagine walking on a half circle to get to the leader's other side
  - Do **NOT** do a [Dile Que No](/moves/dile-que-no) which is very straight
  - On the last count you should turn and connect
---
- During the normal walk roll your shoulders on the free arm
### Cuban Hold Walk
- Starts from [Cuban Hold](/concepts/cuban-hold) [Dile Que No Position](/concepts/dile-que-no-position)
#### Leader
- 123 567 - [Cuban Hold](/concepts/cuban-hold) [Dile Que No](/moves/dile-que-no)
  - 6 - Change to wrist hold (like in Basic Walk)
  - 7 - Step forward
- 123 - Walk to the followers side, switch back to [Cuban Hold](/concepts/cuban-hold)
- 567 - Make her walk
  - Stay on spot
  - Walk can be straight (like [Dile Que No](/moves/dile-que-no)) or half circle
- Repeat until you want to end with [Dile Que No](/moves/dile-que-no)
#### Follower
- 123 567 - [Dile Que No](/moves/dile-que-no)
- 123 - On spot (while the leader is walking)
- 567 - Walk
