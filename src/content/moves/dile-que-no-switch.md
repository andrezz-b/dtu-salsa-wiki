- This is a [Role Switch](/concepts/role-switch) move
- Counting will be done as normal, this may cause the counting to shift by 4 counts after a role switch
### Leader
- 123 - Normal [Dile Que No](/moves/dile-que-no) start from [Dile Que No Position](/concepts/dile-que-no-position)
  - 3 - You should be closer than usual
- 4 - (Optional) Nod towards the follower to indicate the switch
- 5 - Start like [Coca-Cola](/moves/coca-cola), move the hand towards your back instead of spin
  - I am on spot
- 6 - Step forward, you should have put her hand on your back
- 7 - You are out in the circle ready to finish [Dile Que No](/moves/dile-que-no)
  - You are now a *follower*
  - Left hand on the new *leaders* shoulder
  - Give hand in front (if necessary)
### Follower
- 123 - Normal [Dile Que No](/moves/dile-que-no) start
- 56 - Leader should start putting the your hand on his back and stepping in front
- 7 - You are now a *leader*
  - Taker their hand in front
---
- This can be looped, because at the end the current *leader* can just go into another one
