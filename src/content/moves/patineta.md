## ✍️ Description & Execution
### Leader
- 123 - [Enchufla](/moves/enchufla)
- 567 - Send the follower towards the middle of the circle
- 123 567 - Back step
- 123 - No back step, big step forwards (to the left) so you end up behind your next partner
- **Keep Eye Contact with your next follower**
- 567 - Back step
- 123 567 - [Dile Que No](/moves/dile-que-no)
### Follower
- 123 - [Enchufla](/moves/enchufla)
- 567 - Walk around the leader, they should send you towards the middle
- 123 - Walk towards the middle
  - Lift arms from the body and get them horizontal on 3
- 567 123 - Back step
- 567 - [Opposite Vuelta](/moves/opposite-vuelta) towards your new leader
- 123 567 - [Dile Que No](/moves/dile-que-no)
