### Leader
- 567 - Roll in, her right over my head and on left shoulder, my left stays down
  - 123 - Can be literally anything, but you need 2 hands non crossed for the 567
- 1 - Left arm over head (now it's on my left)
  - Also step
- 2 - Step
- 3 - Right arm over head (to my right shoulder)
  - Also step
- 5 - Left arm back over head (back where it came from)
  - Also step
- 6 - Right over head (to my left shoulder)
  - Also step
- 7 - Right over head again, and I turn towards her
- Exit with [Enchufla](/moves/enchufla)
