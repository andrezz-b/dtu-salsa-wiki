## ✍️ Description & Execution
### Leader
- Change grip, my hand over hers, palm down
- 1 - Pull forward (she must be stepping forward, not back as normal)
- 23 - My left gives her hand to my right, offer my left for her other hand
- 5 - Step back
- 67 - Finish turn with the, [Sombrero Finish](/concepts/sombrero-finish)

## ✨ Variations
### Sombrero con Mambo
- While in [Sombrero Finish](/concepts/sombrero-finish) position don't let go and do 1x8 count of [Mambo](/moves/mambo)
