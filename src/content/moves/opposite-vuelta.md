## ✍️ Description & Execution
- Basic left turn (counter clockwise)
### Steps
- 1 - Step forward and prepare by twisting to the right
- 2 - On spot
- 3 - Start turning, you should be facing to the left (90° counter clockwise)
- 5 - Keep turning, you should be facing backwards
- 67 - Finish the turn and face forwards
